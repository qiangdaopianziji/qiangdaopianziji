Trusted Digital Partner

Industry Leading Development. People Centered Design.

Box Clever is an award-winning web design company based in Alberta. For over fifteen years we have been focused on building strong client relationships and industry-leading digital solutions. Our growing team of more than 20 website professionals includes UX/UI Designers, UX Developers, Back-end Developers and more. We tackle projects with innovation, creativity, and well-defined strategies. 

Our Approach

  • 1. Plan

    We listen to your story, ask tough questions, and discover what makes you tick. With insights from stakeholder interviews, analytics, competitor analyses and audience personas, we create goal-oriented strategies that drive success.

  • 2. Build

    Our in-house team puts their talent and expertise to work to craft high-quality, industry leading solutions.



  • 3. Test

    We debug, test and refine, and test some more. Thorough preparation is key for a seamless project launch.

  • 4. Launch

    Every project, no matter the size, is handled with care  - No stress, no downtime, no worries.
